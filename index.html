About Me

Engineer. Leader. Builder.

I joined Hyosung TNS in 2001 and have spent over two decades engineering financial automation software — starting with CD/ATM firmware and growing into full-stack solution development and technical strategy.

Along the way I pursued parallel graduate studies, earning a master's in Information Security (KNOU, 2018) and a second master's in AI Engineering (Kookmin University, 2021), driven by a genuine belief that the best engineers keep learning.

In 2006 I was featured on the cover of Hyosung's company magazine after I independently designed a Power Controller that slashed ATM fault-dispatch calls by 85% — a textbook example of identifying the root cause, selecting the critical fix, and executing it fully. That philosophy still guides my work today.

Currently I bridge engineering and business as part of the Technical Sales Support Team, building ATM SW licence management systems, conducting technology training for the sales force, and analysing SW RFPs to shape competitive proposals.
